About Us

VOK Records

VOK Records was found in 2016 as a group of Vietnamese students who has the same passion
of rap, music, and writing their own songs. The group at first was only doing lo-fi rap,
practiced in writing music, using own words, own stories to share with the audience. The group
member had many members joined in and many had also left after working for a while before choosing
to walk on their own path. The group have grown and improved a lot over time, flexing in produce beats,
songs, mixing, and mastering.
